Our Process

How We Build It

01

Data Audit & Discovery

02

Model Architecture

We select the right approach — from fine-tuned LLMs for conversational AI to gradient-boosted trees for fraud detection — based on your data shape and business goals.

03

Models are trained on your real data, validated against holdout sets, and iteratively refined until accuracy meets production-grade thresholds.

We deploy via scalable APIs with real-time monitoring dashboards, drift detection, and automated retraining pipelines so models stay sharp over time.
