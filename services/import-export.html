The Vision

Why We're Building This

International trade is complex, slow, and paper-heavy. Customs declarations, tariff classifications, compliance checks, and freight coordination still rely on fragmented spreadsheets and manual phone calls. We're building AI that makes cross-border commerce as easy as local delivery — automating documentation, predicting shipping delays, optimizing routes, and ensuring regulatory compliance across jurisdictions before a single container leaves port.

Our Approach

How We're Building It

01

Trade Intelligence Layer

We're training AI models on global trade data — tariff codes, customs regulations, trade agreements — to automate classification and compliance checks across 190+ countries.

02

Document Automation

Bills of lading, certificates of origin, customs declarations, and commercial invoices will be generated automatically from your order and shipment data.

03

Logistics Optimization

AI-driven route planning that factors in shipping costs, transit times, port congestion, weather patterns, and geopolitical risks to recommend the optimal path.

04

Supply Chain Visibility

A real-time dashboard tracking every shipment from origin to destination — with predictive ETAs, exception alerts, and automated stakeholder notifications.

Planned Capabilities

  • Automated HS code classification and duty/tax calculation for any product-country pair
  • One-click generation of all required trade documentation — compliant and audit-ready

  • Real-time shipment tracking with predictive delay alerts and exception management
  • Cross-border compliance engine covering sanctions screening, restricted goods, and trade agreements
